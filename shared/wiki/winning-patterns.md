## Known Meta-Patterns (cross-industry)

These are not yet compiled from our events — they are priors based on general hackathon knowledge.
Update or contradict them as evidence accumulates.

### API-Powered + Visually Immediate
Real data flowing through in real time tends to impress more than static mocks. Judges can see it's real.

### Before / After
Show the user's world before the tool, then after. The contrast makes the value visceral.

### One Specific Person, One Specific Moment
Pitches that name a real-seeming person ("Sarah, an ER nurse at 2am") land harder than abstract use cases.

### Emotionally Resonant Problem
Pain that judges have personally felt or witnessed gets engagement. Abstract efficiency gains do not.
