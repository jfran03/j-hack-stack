# Archetypes Index

> Reusable problem/solution shapes the team returns to.
> An archetype is a shape, not a specific idea — it can be reapplied to a new domain.

---

## What makes something an archetype?

A pattern earns archetype status when:
1. It has appeared in at least two different events or ideas
2. Its core shape (problem → solution mechanism → wow moment) is separable from the specific domain
3. It has a known set of requirements and failure modes
